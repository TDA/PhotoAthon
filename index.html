Rules & Regulations:

1. The contest will be conducted for a span of 3 days.
2. Each participant must submit the photo(s) through mail only.
3. Each participant can submit a maximum of 2 Photos.
4. Photos must be mailed to photoathon@yahoo.com
5. EMAIL’s Subject must be <UNIQUE REG ID> space <Your Name>.
6. Theme for the contest will be “Life in Chennai – Mundane activities but unique to Chennai”
7. All Participants must provide their details along with the photo.
8. The image mailed should be a minimum of 1mb in size and not more than 2mb.
9. NO EXTRAVAGANT editing is allowed, Minor color corrections are accepted.
10. Photos submitted must be within a span of 3 months.
11. Each participant will be given a REG ID at the time of registration in the REG DESK.
12. There will be various REG DESKS all over the city where the participant is to Pay and REGISTER ONLY AFTER THEIR ONLINE REGISTRATION.
13. The REG DESKS will be open for 5 days, starting from FEB 1.
14. Each participant with a valid and correct REG ID will only be considered for evaluation.
15. All students must provide a valid COLLEGE ID card.
16. Registration fees for students will be 100rs per head.
17. Students pursuing any UG and PG degree in any institution can participate in the contest.
18. Judging will be done only based on PHOTOGRAPHY and not based on FB LIKES.
19. Registration fees will be 200rs per head for non-student participants.
20. The top 5 Winners will be intimated on the 5th day from the commencement of the contest.
21. The best 2 photos will be awarded with exuberant prizes.
22. All winners are to submit the original unedited image at the time of prize distribution.

Aims Of Youth Trust ®

Aims of Youth is an organization started by the students for the students. This unique organization has big potential to perform what others aspire. We take pleasure in introducing ourselves as professionals, and you(th) can expect professionalism in each and every step of ours. Though we lack in experience, we have the habit of learning from others mistakes and correct ourselves. India is on the verge of becoming a developed nation in the near future, and there arises the role of students in shaping up the nation and themselves as well. Join the youth army to make a better India for ourselves and for the future generations. Take pride in shaping up the youth India by contributing to the progress of human capital, probably the best capital a smart entrepreneur can as far. The basic inspiration for us for starting the organization is none other than our former President Prof. APJ Abdul Kalam.

The organization’s main idea is to promote education & develop India in 2020 as per president’s dreams. Ambition of our organization is to develop our nation in 2015 i.e. before 2020. We also look forward to walk on the given path 2020 (By Dr. APJ). The basic thought was given by Mr. Nagaraj Sagar K (Founder) after hearing to the inspirational & motivational speeches of Dr. APJ Abdul kalam. Parents can understand & motivate their children’s ideas in a good manner than others likewise; the motivation & encouragement was given to Mr. Nagaraj Sagar K by his Father Mr. Visnu Sagar K. He decided to share his thoughts with his friends & he wanted to have professional students in this organization because of their good thoughts & ideas.
